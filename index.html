Hempel — Color Trends Campaign

I transformed traditional paint swatches into an immersive storytelling experience that resonates with homeowners on a deeper level.

THE CHALLENGE

The goal was to introduce new color trends inspired by nature while making them relatable. I needed to bridge the gap between vast natural elements and the intimate spaces of a modern home.

CREATIVE APPROACH

I developed emotional narratives centered around four themes: Polar, Earth, Space, and Ice. Connecting human emotion with landscapes to envision beauty within walls.
Mastery Academy — Saudi National Day

A digital campaign for Mastery Academy celebrating Saudi National Day, targeting business owners, entrepreneurs, and leaders. The goal was to boost course enrollment and connect with the audience on a personal and national pride level.

THE CHALLENGE

A simple discount wasn't enough to engage the audience during a national occasion. The campaign needed to inspire interest in personal development while making the offer meaningful.

CREATIVE APPROACH

I centered the campaign around the theme of "Creating Your Own Signature." I invited leaders to leave their unique mark on the Kingdom's future, just as their ancestors did. The strategy integrated motivational reels with a key collaboration featuring Nasser Al-Aqeel (Dubamicaffeine), aligning the brand with high-level skill development and intellectual growth.
Vodafone x Ahmed Raafat — Ramadan Campaign

A digital campaign for Vodafone targeting everyday street customers during Ramadan. The campaign offered gifts based on years with the network and aimed to create moments of joy and generosity in the streets.

THE CHALLENGE

Traditional Ramadan campaigns often feel repetitive or staged. The challenge was to capture spontaneous, simple, and authentic moments while filming in public streets, where video quality and participant reactions could be unpredictable.

CREATIVE APPROACH

I chose to target authentic, local neighborhoods to capture the most sincere reactions. I developed a "Lighthearted Interview" framework, writing clever lines and questions that kept the brand present without losing the creator's natural wit. This allowed us to deliver Vodafone's gifts in a way that felt like a shared celebration rather than a corporate giveaway.
Sporti Complex Al Khobar — Family Package

Sporti Complex is a premier sports city in Al Khobar featuring professional courts, fitness centers, and various athletic programs for adults and children. For the Saudi National Day, the campaign aimed to drive sales by targeting parents in the Eastern Province through a dedicated family membership offer.

THE CHALLENGE

Sales data indicated low conversion rates among parents who were prioritizing their children's sports activities while neglecting their own fitness. This behavior resulted in a significant gap in adult memberships. The main challenge was to design an attractive bundle that encourages parents to participate alongside their children rather than just being spectators.

CREATIVE APPROACH

The campaign introduced the Family Package which provides a direct discount when a parent and child subscribe together. The creative direction focused on the strength of the family unit and the mutual encouragement found in training together. We positioned Sporti Complex as the ideal family environment in Al Khobar. By linking national achievements to the unity of the Saudi family, the messaging emphasized that the next national champion starts within your own home.
YOLO System — Educational Campaign

YOLO System is an advanced German-engineered clinic management platform entering the Egyptian market for the first time. The system offers unique technical features designed to streamline medical practices. The campaign aimed to introduce the platform to healthcare professionals across Egypt and provide comprehensive educational content on its sophisticated capabilities.

THE CHALLENGE

The primary challenge was the unfamiliarity of the Egyptian medical community with high-end digital management systems. Physicians were skeptical about the necessity of such advanced technology and the relevance of a German-designed system to the local market needs. There was a clear gap in understanding why a clinic should transition from traditional methods to a specialized digital infrastructure.

CREATIVE APPROACH

Following a strategic pricing adjustment for the Egyptian market, the campaign focused on "Educational Storytelling" to demonstrate the system's value. We produced a dedicated YouTube series titled "Why YOLO" which directly addressed the daily operational struggles of Egyptian doctors. By identifying specific pain points in clinic management and presenting YOLO as the definitive smart solution, the campaign successfully simplified complex technical features into relatable, problem-solving narratives.

YallaNehzar — Entertainment Show

Yalla Nehzar is a creative prank show produced for Shahid, the leading VOD platform in the Middle East. The program's core concept involves participants placing their family members or close friends in awkward or critical situations over the phone to capture their authentic reactions.

THE CHALLENGE

Traditional street pranks often involve high production budgets and significant risks regarding environment control, which can lead to wasted filming days without achieving the desired comedic result. The main challenge was to secure high-quality entertainment and engaging scripts within a controlled setting while ensuring that the safety measures and location choices did not compromise the "surprise" element of the prank.

CREATIVE APPROACH

As the lead scriptwriter and primary ideator, I shifted the focus from random strangers to the participants' inner circles. By involving family members as the "targets," we tapped into pre-existing emotional bonds that generated immediate tension and humor. I designed specific scripts and scenarios that created high-stakes dilemmas through simple phone conversations.
