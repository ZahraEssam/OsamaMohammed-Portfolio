Mastery Academy — Saudi National Day

Vodafone x Ahmed Raafat — Ramadan Campaign

THE CHALLENGE

Traditional Ramadan campaigns often feel repetitive or staged. The challenge was to capture spontaneous, authentic moments while filming in public streets.

Sporti Complex Al Khobar — Family Package

Sporti Complex is a premier sports city in Al Khobar featuring professional courts, fitness centers, and athletic programs for adults and children. The campaign drove sales by targeting parents through a dedicated family membership offer.

THE CHALLENGE

Low conversion rates among parents prioritizing children's sports while neglecting their own fitness. The challenge was designing an attractive bundle encouraging parents to participate alongside their children.

CREATIVE APPROACH

The Family Package gave a direct discount when parent and child subscribe together. We linked national achievements to family unity — the next national champion starts within your own home.

YOLO System — Educational Campaign

YOLO System is an advanced German-engineered clinic management platform entering the Egyptian market. The campaign introduced the platform to healthcare professionals and provided comprehensive educational content on its capabilities.

THE CHALLENGE

The Egyptian medical community was unfamiliar with high-end digital management systems. Physicians were skeptical about advanced technology and the relevance of a German-designed system to local market needs.

CREATIVE APPROACH

The campaign focused on "Educational Storytelling" — a dedicated YouTube series titled "Why YOLO" addressing daily operational struggles of Egyptian doctors and presenting YOLO as the definitive smart solution.

YallaNehzar — Entertainment Show

Yalla Nehzar is a creative prank show produced for Shahid, the leading VOD platform in the Middle East. The program involves participants placing family members in awkward situations over the phone to capture authentic reactions.

THE CHALLENGE

Traditional street pranks involve high production budgets and significant risks. The challenge was securing high-quality entertainment within a controlled setting without compromising the "surprise" element.

CREATIVE APPROACH

As lead scriptwriter, I shifted focus from random strangers to participants' inner circles. By involving family members as "targets," we tapped pre-existing emotional bonds generating immediate tension and humor.
